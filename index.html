Who are the female directors that didn't make it into the BBC's greatest movies list?

Back in 2016, 177 movie critics from all around the world (actually, 82 from the US) chose what they considered the best 10 movies of the 21st Century. In the end, only the 100 most voted made it into a final list. A list with only 11 movies directed or co-directed by women. So we wondered: what other movies were selected but simply didn't appear on the final ranking? And lastly, do those movies -in general- portray more nuanced female characters, according to the Bechdel test?

For seaweed farmers in Zanzibar, climate change is a current threat

Seaweed farming, a sector wounded to death by climate change, represents the lifeline of some 23,000 women in the Tanzanian archipelago of Zanzibar. However, in recent years, good harvests have become less and less common due to much warmer superficial waters of up to 38 degrees Celsius. Pests and diseases seem to be the new normal.
